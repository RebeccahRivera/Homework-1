New York City

About NYC

New York City popularly called the City that never sleeps is located at the southern tip of the State of New York, the city is the center of the New York metropolitan area, the largest metropolitan area in the world by urban area.

Weather

NYC summers are hot and humid while winters are very cold and windy but partly cloudy year round.
